What is Saigon Cup?

Born in 2014 with the mission to improve the Vietnamese fighting game scene, the Street Fighter Vietnam community based in Hồ Chí Minh City worked together to organize an international fighting game tournament right in Vietnam - a feat that no other domestic organizer managed to achieve. With 5 years of experience under our belt, Saigon Cup 2019 is endorsed by Capcom and SNK, being an official event in both Capcom Pro Tour and Neo Geo World Tour (King of Fighters).

Whether you're a fighting game lover or just a curious spectator wanting to learn more about the scene, Saigon Cup is the place to be.

Rules & Regulations

1. All 5 tournaments will use the double elimination format, played on SONY PlayStation 4.

2. All matches will be BO3 (Best Of 3). Street Fighter V top 8 (Single) matches will be BO5. Winners Finals, Losers Finals and Grand Finals for Tekken, Guilty Gear & Dragon Ball will also be BO5.

3. In Grand Finals, the player from Losers bracket will need to win 2 sets to win the tournament.

4. Players can register at the venue on tournament day. However, it is advisable to register online in advance so we can provide you with a better tournament experience.

5. Brackets will be announced 3 days before tournament day on Saigon FGC's fanpage.

6. Gaming equipment will be provided by the tournament organizers but players should still bring their own. Converters are allowed.

7. Smoking is not allowed at the tournament venue to ensure a healthy environment for players.

Prizes

For Street Fighter V (Single), in addition to prize money & merchandise, top 8 players will earn Capcom Pro Tour points. More details on prizes will be updated Soon™.

Prizes are as follows:

  • Street Fighter V: CPT points for top 8; 5,000,000 VND for first place.
  • Street Fighter V (3v3): 3,000,000 VND for first place.
  • King of Fighters XIV: 12,000,000 VND + air ticket to Hong Kong Neo Geo World Tour Finals for first place.
  • King of Fighters' 98: 12,000,000 VND + air ticket to Hong Kong Neo Geo World Tour Finals for first place.
  • Dragon Ball FighterZ: 4,000,000 VND for first place.
